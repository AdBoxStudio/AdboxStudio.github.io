Biography

Farbod Jafarpour is an internationally recognized creative director and visual strategist with over 15 years of experience crafting compelling visual narratives for luxury fashion, commercial, and e-commerce brands.

As the founder of Adbox Studio, Farbod bridges the gap between artistic vision and commercial strategy, delivering visually stunning content that drives engagement and conversion. His work is characterized by a minimalist aesthetic, meticulous attention to detail, and a deep understanding of brand storytelling.

Based between Dubai and Tehran, Farbod has collaborated with some of the most prestigious brands in the Middle East and internationally, bringing a unique perspective that blends contemporary Western influences with rich Middle Eastern visual heritage.
